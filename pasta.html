Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Prep Time:

10 min

Cook Time:

25 min

Difficulty:

Medium

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine (or any pasta)
  • 1 chicken breast (or 2 thighs)
  • Salt + pepper + garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or half-and-half)
  • 1 cup parmesan (grated)
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water, then drain (save a little pasta water).
  2. Season chicken with salt/pepper/garlic powder.
  3. Cook chicken in pan until done (about 4-6 min each side), then slice.
  4. In same pan, melt butter, add garlic (optional), then add cream.
  5. Add parmesan and stir until smooth.
  6. Add pasta + chicken. If too thick, add a splash of pasta water.

Egg Sandwich

Prep Time:

5 min

Cook Time:

7 min

Difficulty:

Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices bread (or 1 bun)
  • 1 slice cheese (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt + pepper
  • (Optional) mayo / ketchup / hot sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Crack eggs in a bowl, add salt + pepper, mix.
  2. Heat pan on medium, add butter/oil.
  3. Pour eggs and scramble (or cook as one folded omelet).
  4. Toast bread.
  5. Add eggs + cheese to bread.
  6. Add sauce if you want, then close sandwich.

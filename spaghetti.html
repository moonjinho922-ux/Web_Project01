Spaghetti w Meatballs

Prep Time:

15 min

Cook Time:

35 min

Difficulty:

Medium

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 10–12 frozen meatballs (or homemade)
  • 1 jar marinara sauce (24 oz)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt
  • Parmesan (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil water, add salt, cook spaghetti (follow box time).
  2. In another pan, warm marinara sauce on medium-low.
  3. Add meatballs to sauce, cover, simmer 15–20 min (until hot).
  4. Drain pasta, mix with sauce (or plate pasta then add meatballs + sauce).
  5. Top with parmesan.

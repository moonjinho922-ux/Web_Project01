Mac & Cheese

Prep Time:

15 min

Cook Time:

30 min

Difficulty:

Medium

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar best)
  • Salt + pepper
  • (Optional) paprika or hot sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook macaroni, drain.
  2. Melt butter in pot on medium.
  3. Add flour, stir 1 min (makes a paste).
  4. Slowly pour milk in while stirring (no lumps).
  5. Stir until it thickens (3–5 min).
  6. Turn heat low, add cheese, mix until melted.
  7. Add macaroni, mix, season with salt/pepper.
